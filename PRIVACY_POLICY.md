# GOA UNIVERSE Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

GOA UNIVERSE respects your privacy.

The app processes audio files locally on your device. When you choose an audio file, the app analyzes tempo, key, and frequency balance to generate a Goa trance pattern.

## Data Collection

GOA UNIVERSE does not collect personal data.

The app does not use:
- User accounts
- Tracking
- Remote servers

Selected audio files stay on your device. They are not uploaded by the app.

## Advertising

GOA UNIVERSE uses Google AdMob to show banner ads. Google may process information such as device identifiers, ad interactions, diagnostics, and approximate ad delivery data to provide and measure ads.

GOA UNIVERSE does not upload your selected audio files to Google or to any app server.

## File Access

The app only accesses audio files you choose through the system file picker. This access is used to analyze the file and generate music parameters.
